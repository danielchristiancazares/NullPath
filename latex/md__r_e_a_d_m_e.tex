Null\+Path is a CUDA/\+C++ prototype that integrates photon paths (null geodesics) in curved spacetime and renders black‑hole imagery. It includes an RK4 integrator, precomputed deflection/bending tables, a thin‑disk renderer with progressive accumulation, and sanity tests. Schwarzschild is the validated core; Kerr support is experimental (finite‑difference metric derivatives pending analytic forms).\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md49}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Quickstart}{Quickstart}}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md49}
